The Science of Balance

Understanding Ayurveda

Ayurveda, an ancient Indian system rooted in Vedic wisdom, seeks to harmonize the three doshas — Vata, Pitta and Kapha — for overall well-being. Its personalized approach considers individual constitutions, employing herbs like turmeric and ashwagandha to restore balance. The holistic practices of Ayurveda, including lifestyle adjustments and dietary changes, aim to address the root causes of ailments rather than just their symptoms.
